Sustainability

Our foundational principles of “Operating Responsibly” and “Growing Sustainably” are the cornerstone of our corporate values as we navigate through the intricate terrain of the pharmaceutical industry. Our obligations extend well beyond our offices, impacting the lives of people and communities across the globe. With this in mind, we strive for sustainable expansion through innovative approaches that intertwine economic advancement, environmental preservation, and social well-being.

KNOW MORE arrow_forward

We have set a target to reduce our Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions by 42% by FY2030 - in line with the 1.5°C pathway suggested by the Science-Based Targets Initiative.

Peter DeYoung

CEO, Piramal Global Pharma

As a responsible corporate entity, while we are aware of our responsibility to deliver shareholder value through profitable growth, we are equally conscious of our role in integrating sustainable practices into our operations.

Nandini Piramal

Chairperson, Piramal Pharma Limited
