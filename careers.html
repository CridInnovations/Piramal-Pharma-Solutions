Piramal Pharma Solutions thrives in an environment of constant discovery, learning, and enthusiasm. Learning Opportunities

#SeekToGrow by investing in your personal and professional growth.

At Piramal Pharma Solutions, we have a very strong focus on learning. Knowledge being one of our four Values, there has always been a strong focus on learning and development of our employees. We have established our own Piramal Learning University, which includes various academies like the Leadership Academy and functional academies like HR Academy, Finance Academy, GBSS Shared Services Academy (Gurukul), IT Academy, Quality Academy, Project Management Academy, etc. We also have our Piramal Learning University – Virtual Campus to provide digital learning opportunities with cutting edge learning content.

There are various flagship programs for skill building, like Summit, Ascend, Ignite, Piramal Leadership Series, Discover Piramal, STEP – Strive to Excellence Program, Global Leaders Connect Sessions, etc., to address the current and future skilling needs of our talent.
